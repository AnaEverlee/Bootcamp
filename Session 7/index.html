Get To Know Me

I'm currently living in the Philippines and studying Information Technology in De La Salle University Dasmarinas. If I could describe myself using three words, it would be innovative, reticent, but bold whenthesituationcallsforit. Oops, I think I surpassed three words.

These are only a few of the many things I love...

Reading

Picking up a book sometimes takes grueling effort and on top of that, I'm a really slow reader. Once I break the curse, however, I enjoy the sweet escapism of getting to know fictional characters and the lives that unfold before them, pre-written by a God, the author of their stories.

Playing Sports

I used to train competitively for swimming (though it was just for a few months) and during our school's intramurals, I'd compete in Track and Field. The months, weeks, and days building up to the competition day gives me thrill and motivates me to challenge myself not only physically but also mentally.

Travelling

I've visited Japan, Thailand, and Malaysia in the past years (and in less than a month, Switzerland!). My favorite part of exploring different countries is witnessing their beautiful landscapes and unique culture. Of course, the food is the best part!
